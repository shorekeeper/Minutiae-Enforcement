MEMinutiae Enforcement

Sanctions with a paper trail.

Minutiae Enforcement is a moderation engine for Paper servers. Every sanction cites a rule, records its precedent, escalates by a declared ladder, and can be lifted, pardoned, or vacated with the distinction preserved. No silent state, no untraceable bans.

# A layout carries the rule, duration, ladder, and defaults:
/enforce Steve ::griefing

# Or spell everything out by hand:
/enforce Steve @measure(MUTE) duration=3h reason="Flooding chat"

# Lifts carry intent. A pardon keeps the record in precedent;
# a vacatur erases it:
/enforce lift 63 pardon @note("Resolved in ticket 412")

# A rule was repealed? Preview, then vacate everything under it:
/enforce liftall rule P.5.1
/enforce liftall rule P.5.1 confirm

One grammar, everywhere.

Sanctions are issued through a compact token grammar: ::layout selects a configured template, @flag applies an annotation, key=value overrides a scalar. The same tokens work in commands, configuration files, and automation, and pass through identical validation and permission checks.

Tab completion is context-sensitive: after a target it offers layouts and measures, inside @notify( it offers your configured channels, after a temporal measure it offers durations.

What it does

The parts most ban plugins leave to spreadsheets and tribal knowledge.

Precedent that means something

Escalation ladders consult a player's prior record under the cited rule. Vacated sanctions leave precedent; pardoned ones stay. Decay windows age old offences out automatically, and probation windows aggravate repeat offences.

Lifts are not all equal

Vacate a wrongful ban, pardon a deserved one, or close it as time served. Cascade over joined cases, bulk-vacate a repealed rule, reverse a mistaken lift. Every path lands in the audit log.

Ban evasion detection

A calibrated Bayesian model scores incoming connections against the signals of banned accounts and explains every flag signal by signal. Alerts only; staff decide.

Localized end to end

Every player-facing line, measure name, duration, and staff rank renders in the recipient's own locale, including grammatical case where the language needs it. Bundles are plain YAML.

Web case browser

A registry-style panel navigates subjects, sanctions, appeals, chat transcripts, and audit trails as one tree. Read and write roles are separate tokens with bounded authority.

Built-in appeals

Players appeal with /appeal; staff review a queue, accept or deny with a reason, and an accepted appeal vacates the sanction through the same audited path as a manual lift.

How it fits together

1

Write your rules once

Server rules live in rules.yml as numbered entries (P.3.2). Every sanction cites one; a layout naming a missing rule refuses to load rather than failing silently.

2

Configure layouts

A layout in layouts.yml binds a rule to a measure, duration, escalation ladder, and default annotations. Layouts inherit from private bases, so shared policy is written once.

3

Moderate

Staff type /enforce Player ::layout. The engine consults precedent, applies the ladder, persists the case, notifies channels, and broadcasts in each viewer's language.
