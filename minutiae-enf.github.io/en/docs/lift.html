Lifting sanctions

Removing a sanction is not one operation. The engine distinguishes why a sanction ends, because the answer decides whether the record keeps counting against the player.

The three kinds

Every lift carries a kind. It is recorded permanently, shown in the sanction's status, and consulted by every future escalation decision.

KindMeaningPrecedentNode
vacate The sanction was wrongly issued. Erased. Future offences escalate as if it never existed. covered by minutiae.command.lift
pardon Rightly issued, remitted as clemency. Kept. A repeat offence escalates on top of it. minutiae.lift.pardon
served Terminated early as fulfilled; an early expiry. Kept. minutiae.lift.served

When no kind is given, the lift is a vacatur. This matches the common case of undoing a mistake and the behaviour of every lift made before kinds existed.

Warning

Vacating out of sympathy is the classic mistake. A player who earned the mute but talked their way out of it should be pardoned, not vacated; otherwise their next offence starts the escalation ladder from zero.

Command form

/enforce lift <id> [vacate|pardon|served] [@annotation...] [reason...]

Bare tokens after the kind become the reason. Annotations may be interleaved anywhere in the remainder:

/enforce lift 63 pardon Behaved well on appeal @note("Ticket 412")
/enforce lift 80 served @silent
/enforce lift 91 @dry-run @cascade

Admissible annotations

@silent Suppress the public lift announcement. The actor's own confirmation still prints.
@dry-run Preview: reports what would be lifted, including cascade extent, and changes nothing.
@note(text) Attach an internal case note to the lifted sanction.
@notify(ch...) Route a lift notification to the named channels.
@probation(2w) Open a probation window on the lifted record: a repeat offence inside it is aggravated. Rejected for a vacate, whose record leaves precedent and cannot carry a visible window.
@cascade Extend the lift over the whole joinder chain. Requires minutiae.lift.cascade.

Target-dependent gates

Two checks depend on the sanction being lifted, not on the command, and therefore apply to every entry path: the command, appeal acceptance, and the web panel.

minutiae.lift.foreign Required to lift a sanction issued by someone else. Without it, staff can only undo their own work. Note that ownership follows the attributed name: a sanction issued with @as(Alice) belongs to Alice for this gate.
minutiae.lift.blocking Required to lift a suspension or a permanent ban. Without it, staff can lift mutes, quarantines, and warnings only.

Cascading over joined cases

Sanctions woven under a parent with @weave(#id) share precedent as one case. A plain lift removes one member and leaves the rest standing. With @cascade, every unlifted member of the chain is lifted under the same kind and reason, and the actor receives an aggregate count.

/enforce lift 42 vacate @dry-run @cascade
  Dry-Run: would lift #42 Steve SUSPENSION (vacated)
    Cascade would additionally lift 3 woven case(s)

/enforce lift 42 vacate @cascade Wrong player identified

The foreign and blocking gates are evaluated against the chain root; the cascade permission itself is the senior authority for the members.

Bulk lifts

An amnesty over a criterion rather than an identifier. Two criteria exist: a rule (the rule was repealed) and a staff name (an issuer's work must be undone wholesale). Bulk lifts are always vacaturs and require minutiae.lift.bulk.

/enforce liftall rule P.5.1
  17 sanction(s) match 'P.5.1' - append confirm to execute

/enforce liftall rule P.5.1 confirm Rule repealed 2025-01-10
/enforce liftall staff BadActor confirm

The bare form is always a preview; nothing executes without the literal confirm. A bulk lift covers active, stayed, and suspended sanctions in one transaction and appends a single aggregate audit entry.

Note

On a multi-server deployment, affected players online on another instance are reconciled on their next join. Players online on the executing instance reconcile immediately.

Reversing a mistaken lift

/enforce unlift <id> [reason...]

Requires minutiae.lift.reinstate. The lift metadata is cleared and the sanction returns to force: behavioural constraints re-apply to an online subject, and a restored ban removes them from the server. A sanction whose duration expired while lifted stays inactive; time did not stop.

Note

A sanction that was stayed or suspended at lift time returns as plainly active: the lift-unlift round trip consumes its deferral window. The audit entry records the reinstatement.

Appeals and lifting

Accepting an appeal lifts the sanction as a vacatur - the appellate finding is that the sanction should not stand as issued. If the finding is instead that the player has earned clemency, deny the appeal and lift manually with pardon.

What gets recorded

Every path writes an audit entry: LIFT with the actor and reason, BULK_LIFT with the criterion and count, UNLIFT with the reinstatement reason. The kind lives on the sanction row itself and is visible in /enforce info, the docket, and the web panel's case tree.
